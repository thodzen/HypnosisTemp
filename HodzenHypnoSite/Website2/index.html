About Me

Michael (Mick) Hodzen is a retired federal agent with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Psychology and Criminal Justice from Michigan State University. In 1990, he was certified in Investigative and Forensic Hypnosis from the Therapeutic and Forensic Hypnosis Institute of Texas, Alamo Area Law Enforcement Academy at the University of Texas at San Antonio. In 2018, Michael was certified as a Clinical and Transpersonal Hypnotherapist at the Institute of Interpersonal Hypnotherapy, Tampa, Florida. This training included 500 hours of state-licensed hypnotherapy training. Michael is also a member of the International Society of Interpersonal Hypnotherapists.
